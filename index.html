🌱 Smart Plant Communicator

A self-watering plant that texts you when it's thirsty. NodeMCU ESP8266 + soil sensor + water-level probe + pump + Telegram. Everything you need to build it, simulate it, and tune it — in one place.

Plant Sim IDE

Paste your sketch, click Run, watch the plant get watered. The IDE parses your tuning constants and drives a live animated simulation — perfect for testing thresholds and timings without burning out a real pump.

What's in the source folders

  • plant_communicator/plant_communicator.ino — production sketch for the real NodeMCU. Real-world timings (60 s sample, 1 h cooldown, 6 pumps/day cap).
  • wokwi/ — Wokwi simulator project files (ESP32 and Wemos D1 Mini variants) so you can verify the firmware in your browser before flashing hardware.
  • plant-sim-ide/ — the visual IDE in this hub.

All the source is on GitHub. Fork, modify, deploy your own — it's a static site, no backend, no build step.
